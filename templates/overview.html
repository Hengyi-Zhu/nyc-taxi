Overview

The following graphs are created using the entire data available on Uber trips and the data during the same time periods for yellow and green taxi. We can see that the total number of trips taken during those time periods fluctuates, but the percentage of Uber trips is steadily increasing over time. This suggests that Uber is rapidly growing, with many taxi trips, perhaps taxi drivers as well, converted to Uber.
