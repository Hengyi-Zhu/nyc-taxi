Introduction

Goals

  • To provide some helpful information - things that I wish I could know - to the general public. How does the demand for taxi change during the day? Where are the taxis if you often have trouble getting one? And how much do people usually tip or am I over or under tipping? Data, Overview, Location, Time, Fare
  • To offer a simple web application to let people select pickup and dropoff locations, date and time you plan to take the trip, and then return a machine learning predicted cab fare and length of trip in minutes. How Long Will My Trip Take & How Much Will It Cost

Tools

  • Data was downloaded from the TLC website (streamlined with Python) and Github. Detailed descriptions of data are available on the data section.
  • Data was then processed in PostgreSQL to produce manageably sized subsets or samples.
  • Analysis was done using Ipython Notebook. Major packages include pandas, numpy.
  • Visualizations were done with Bokeh and CartoDB.
  • Regressions and machine learning algorithms were implemented using scikit-learn and statsmodels.
  • This website was created with Flask and Bootstrap.
