Fare

The data used for this section is created by combining random samples of size 10000 for each month in 2015 for both green and yellow cabs. Outliers (possibly due to inaccurate data entry) are removed. Since tips by cash are not recorded, we only keep the trips paid by credit cards so we have the complete information.

Components

Since 2015, there are six possible components for a taxi trip bill.

Visit Taxicab Rate of Fare for more detailed descriptions of these components.

Tips

The graph below shows the distribution of tips percentage. The median is 20% and the mean is 17%~18%.

The original sample contains the same number of yellow and green taxi trips. However, once trips paid by cash are eliminated, significantly more yellow taxi trips remain. Therefore, we know borough (green) taxi users tend to pay by cash more.

Tolls

More than 95% trips do not involve tolls. For the rest of the trips, the most common toll amount is $5.54, for crossing one of these: Robert F. Kennedy (Triborough) Bridge, Throgs Neck Bridge, Bronx–Whitestone Bridge, Queens Midtown Tunnel, Hugh L. Carey (Brooklyn–Battery) Tunnel.
