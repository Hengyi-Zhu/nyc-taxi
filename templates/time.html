Time

Trip distribution by the hour:

The following two graphs show the distribution of all the trips taken place between 2015-01-01 and 2015-06-31 for each type of taxi during different hours. We can identify 7pm as the peak hour for taxi usage.

Trip distribution by day of the week:

The following two graphs show the distribution of all the trips taken place between 2015-01-01 and 2015-06-31 for each type of taxi during different days of the week (0 - 6 with 0 as Sunday, 1 as Monday, ... ). Mondays and Saturdays are the least and the most busy times for the taxi drivers, respectively.
