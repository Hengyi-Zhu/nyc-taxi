Location

The following graphs are generated from the pickup and dropoff locations of Uber, yellow cab, and green cab between 2014-04-01 and 2014-09-30. Feel free to toggle the visible layers of the graphs (in this case, different layers represent different cab types).

We are restricted to this time period because of the limited availablity of Uber data. In addition, only pickup locations are available for Uber. Since the entire dataset is too large, I collected random samples of size 10000 for each month/cab type and concatenated them to produce these graphs.

Some interesting observations include:

  • Yellow cabs dominate the JFK airport business
  • Uber covers the widest pickup area
  • The Bronx relies more heavily on the green cabs than the other outer boroughs do
  • ...
